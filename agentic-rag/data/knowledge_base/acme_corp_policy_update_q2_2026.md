# Acme Corporation Policy Update - Q2 2026

Document Type: Company Policy Update  
Company: Acme Corporation  
Policy Effective Date: 2026-04-01  
Version: 2.0

---

## Purpose

This policy update introduces revised operating, leave, and compliance rules for Acme Corporation. These updates supersede any conflicting provisions in prior policy documents.

---

## 1. Work Model and Attendance (Updated)

- Standard work week remains Monday to Friday.
- Employees must be available during core collaboration hours: 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time.
- Eligible employees may work remotely up to 4 days per week with manager approval.
- Team attendance day is mandatory every Wednesday for hybrid teams.
- Timesheet submission deadline is now end of day Friday each week.

---

## 2. Leave Policy (Updated)

### 2.1 Annual Leave
- Full-time employees receive 22 paid annual leave days per calendar year.
- Annual leave accrual is monthly from date of joining.
- Up to 12 unused annual leave days can be carried forward.
- Leave requests of 3 or more consecutive days must be submitted at least 7 calendar days in advance.

### 2.2 Sick Leave
- Employees receive 14 paid sick leave days annually.
- Medical documentation is required for sick leave longer than 2 consecutive work days.

### 2.3 Wellness Day
- Employees receive 2 paid wellness days per year.
- Wellness days do not carry forward to the next calendar year.

---

## 3. Information Security and AI Usage (New)

- Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for all company systems.
- Password rotation interval is updated to every 120 days.
- Uploading customer data, source code, or confidential HR records into public AI tools is prohibited.
- Only company-approved AI tools may be used for business workflows.
- Suspected security incidents must be reported within 30 minutes to it-security@acmecorp.com.

---

## 4. HR Operations and Compliance (Updated)

- Mandatory compliance training must be completed within 15 days of assignment.
- Managers must complete probation review checkpoints at day 30, day 60, and day 90.
- Internal transfer requests require a minimum of 9 months in current role unless CHRO exception is approved.
- All policy violations are triaged by HR within 2 business days.

---

## 5. Expense and Reimbursement (Updated)

- Expense claims must be submitted within 7 business days of expense date.
- Claims above USD 500 require manager pre-approval reference.
- Missing receipts above USD 25 require a signed declaration form.
- Reimbursements are processed in the next payroll cycle after approval.

---

## 6. Enforcement

Failure to comply with this policy may result in corrective action, including temporary access restrictions, formal warnings, or further disciplinary measures consistent with company HR procedures.

---

## 7. Acknowledgement

All employees are required to review and acknowledge this policy update through the HR portal within 10 business days of publication.
