## 5. Leave Policy

### 5.1 Annual / Privilege Leave
- All full-time employees are entitled to **20 days of paid annual leave** per calendar year.
- Leave accrues at **1.67 days per month** from the date of joining.
- Unused leave of up to **10 days** may be carried forward to the next year.
- Annual leave must be pre-approved by the employee's direct manager at least **3 business days** in advance.

### 5.2 Sick Leave
- Employees are entitled to **12 days of paid sick leave** per year.
- For absences exceeding **3 consecutive days**, a medical certificate is required.
- Sick leave does not carry forward to the following year.

### 5.3 Casual Leave
- **6 days of casual leave** per year for unexpected personal matters.
- Maximum **2 consecutive casual leave days** permitted at a time.
- Casual leave cannot be combined with annual leave without manager approval.

### 5.4 Maternity Leave
- **26 weeks (6 months) of fully paid maternity leave** for the birth or adoption of a child.
- Applicable from the first day of employment.
- An additional **4 weeks of unpaid leave** may be requested.

### 5.5 Paternity Leave
- **2 weeks (10 working days) of fully paid paternity leave** upon birth or adoption of a child.
- Must be taken within 3 months of the child's birth/adoption.

### 5.6 Bereavement Leave
- **5 paid days** for the death of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling).
- **3 paid days** for extended family (grandparents, in-laws).

### 5.7 Marriage Leave
- **3 days of paid marriage leave** for the employee's own marriage.
- Must be applied for at least **2 weeks** in advance.

### 5.8 Public Holidays
- Employees observe all **national public holidays** as per the country/state of work location.
- Typically **10–12 public holidays** per year.

### 5.9 Compensatory Off (Comp-Off)
- Employees who work on a public holiday or weekend (when officially required) are entitled to a comp-off day within **30 days**.

### 5.10 Unpaid Leave
- Unpaid leave may be granted at management discretion for extended personal circumstances.

## 6. Work Hours & Attendance Policy

- **Standard working hours:** 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM (Monday to Friday), totaling **45 hours per week**.
- **Flexible work hours:** Employees may opt into a flextime window of 8:00 AM – 10:00 AM start, with corresponding end times, subject to manager approval.
- **Remote work:** Employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week** (role-dependent).
- Core collaboration hours: **10:00 AM – 4:00 PM** must be observed by all team members regardless of flex arrangements.
- **Overtime:** Pre-approved overtime is compensated at **1.5x the normal hourly rate** for non-exempt employees.

---

## 7. Compensation & Benefits

### 7.1 Salary & Pay Cycle
- Salaries are paid on the **last working day of each month**.
- Pay slips are issued digitally via the HR portal.

### 7.2 Performance Bonus
- Annual performance bonuses ranging from **5% – 30%** of base salary based on individual and company performance ratings.
- Bonuses are disbursed in **March** each year for the previous year's performance.

### 7.3 Health Insurance
- All full-time employees and their **immediate dependents (spouse + 2 children)** are covered under the company's group health insurance plan.
- Coverage includes hospitalization, outpatient, dental, and vision.
- Premium is **fully covered by Acme Corporation**.

### 7.4 Provident Fund / Retirement
- Acme contributes **12% of basic salary** to the employee's provident fund (or equivalent national pension scheme).
- Employee also contributes 12% of basic salary.

### 7.5 Employee Stock Options (ESOP)
- Senior and long-tenure employees are eligible for ESOPs with a **4-year vesting schedule** (1-year cliff).

### 7.6 Internet & Home Office Allowance
- Remote employees receive a **$50/month internet allowance** and a one-time **$500 home office setup allowance**.

### 7.7 Meal & Transport Allowance
- Office-based employees receive a **$150/month meal allowance** and **$100/month transport allowance**.

## 8. Performance Management

- **Review Cycle:** Bi-annual — Mid-year review in **July** and Annual review in **December/January**.
- **Ratings Scale:** Exceptional (5) → Exceeds Expectations (4) → Meets Expectations (3) → Needs Improvement (2) → Unsatisfactory (1)
- **Performance Improvement Plan (PIP):** Employees rated 1 or 2 consecutively may be placed on a 90-day PIP with coaching and clear goals.
- **Promotion Cycle:** Promotions are considered during the annual review cycle; employees must have at least **12 months in their current role**.

---

## 9. Learning & Development

- **Annual L&D Budget:** Each employee receives **$1,500 per year** for external training, certifications, or courses.
- **Internal Training:** Monthly skill-sharing sessions, workshops, and leadership bootcamps.
- **Mentorship Program:** All new hires are assigned a senior mentor for their first 6 months.
- **Tuition Reimbursement:** Up to **$5,000/year** for job-related graduate programs, subject to manager approval and continued employment obligation.
- **Certifications:** Company sponsors relevant industry certifications (AWS, GCP, PMI, etc.) upon manager approval.

## 10. Onboarding Policy

- **Pre-boarding:** Offer letter, equipment dispatch, and systems access provisioned **5 business days before Day 1**.
- **Day 1:** Orientation, ID card, system logins, team introductions, HR briefing.
- **Week 1:** Department-specific onboarding, tool training, meet-the-leadership sessions.
- **30-60-90 Day Plan:** Every new hire has structured goals for 30, 60, and 90 days.
- **Buddy Program:** Each new hire is paired with a buddy from their team for informal support.
- **Probation Period:** 6-month probation for all new employees; reviewed by HR and manager at 3 and 6 months.

## 13. IT & Data Security Policy

- Employees must use **company-issued devices** for work-related tasks.
- All devices must have **full-disk encryption** and updated antivirus software.
- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** is mandatory for all company systems.
- Employees must not install unauthorized software on company devices.
- Passwords must be at least **12 characters**, include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols, and be changed every **90 days**.
- Any security incident or suspected breach must be reported to it-security@acmecorp.com **within 1 hour** of discovery.

## 14. Remote Work Policy

- Up to **3 days per week** remote work is permitted for eligible roles.
- Employees must maintain a professional, distraction-free workspace.
- Core hours (**10 AM – 4 PM** local time) must be observed for availability.
- VPN is mandatory when accessing company systems remotely.
- Full-remote roles are approved on a case-by-case basis and require VP-level sign-off.

## 15. Travel & Expense Policy

- Business travel must be pre-approved via the travel management portal.
- Domestic travel: Economy class flights, hotel up to **$150/night**.
- International travel: Economy class (flights under 8 hours), Business class (8+ hours), hotel up to **$200/night**.
- Meal expenses: Up to **$75/day** while traveling.
- All expenses must be submitted with receipts within **10 business days** of the trip.

## 16. Grievance & Escalation Policy

- Employees can raise a grievance formally via the **HR portal** or email hr@acmecorp.com.
- All grievances are acknowledged within **48 hours** and resolved within **15 business days**.
- Employees may escalate to the CHRO if not satisfied with the resolution.
- Whistleblower protections are in place; retaliation against employees who raise complaints in good faith is strictly prohibited.

---

## 17. Resignation & Exit Policy

- **Notice Period:**
  - Junior employees (0–3 years): 30 days
  - Mid-level employees (3–7 years): 45 days
  - Senior employees (7+ years): 60 days
- Exit interviews are conducted by HR within the last week.
- Full and final settlement (salary, gratuity, reimbursements) is processed within **30 days** of the last working day.
- Employees must return all company assets (laptop, ID card, access cards) on or before the last working day.
