# Working Hours and Attendance Policy

## Standard Working Hours

### Full-Time Employees
- **Daily Hours**: 9 hours (including 1-hour lunch break)
- **Work Hours**: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
- **Flexible Start Time**: 8:30 AM to 10:00 AM (with corresponding end time adjustment)
- **Weekly Hours**: 45 hours (Monday to Friday)

### Flexible Working Options
- **Core Hours**: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM (mandatory presence)
- **Flex Hours**: Employees can adjust start/end times around core hours
- **Prior Approval**: Flexible arrangements require manager approval

## Attendance Requirements

### Punctuality
- Employees are expected to arrive on time
- Late arrivals (after 10:00 AM without flex approval) are noted
- More than 3 late arrivals per month may result in warning

### Break Times
- **Lunch Break**: 1 hour (12:00 PM to 2:00 PM window)
- **Tea Breaks**: Two 15-minute breaks allowed
- **Total Work Time**: 8 hours of productive work expected daily

## Remote Work Policy

### Eligibility
- Available after completing 3 months of employment
- Department head approval required
- Based on role suitability and performance

### Guidelines
- Maximum 2 days per week (unless special circumstances)
- Core hours must be maintained
- Full availability on communication channels required
- Deliverables and productivity expectations unchanged

## Overtime Policy

### Overtime Eligibility
- Applicable for non-managerial staff
- Requires prior approval from supervisor
- Calculated at 1.5x regular hourly rate

### Overtime Limits
- Maximum 10 hours per week
- Must not exceed 40 hours per month
- Compensatory off can be taken instead of overtime pay (with approval)

## Weekend and Holiday Work

### Weekend Work
- Typically Saturday and Sunday are off
- If work required, prior approval needed
- Compensation: Double pay OR compensatory off

### Public Holidays
- All declared public holidays are off
- List published at beginning of each year
- No work expected on public holidays unless critical

## Time Tracking

### Attendance System
- Biometric or digital check-in/check-out mandatory
- Employees must mark attendance personally
- Proxy attendance is strictly prohibited

### Timesheet Submission
- Required for project-based work
- Weekly submission deadline: Every Monday 11 AM
- Approval by project manager required

## Half-Day and Short-Leave

### Half-Day
- **Duration**: 4 hours of work
- **Leave Deduction**: 0.5 day from leave balance
- **Advance Notice**: 1 day required

### Short Leave
- **Duration**: Up to 2 hours
- **Frequency**: Maximum 2 times per month
- **Approval**: Immediate supervisor approval
- **No Deduction**: If used within monthly limits

## Work from Home (WFH) During Special Circumstances

### Emergency WFH
- Weather emergencies, strikes, personal emergencies
- Manager notification required
- Full availability and productivity expected

### Medical WFH
- Available for minor illnesses
- Medical certificate may be required for more than 2 days
- Full-day sick leave can be taken instead if unable to work

## Consequences of Non-Compliance

### Unauthorized Absence
- Absence without intimation: Marked as "absent without leave"
- Salary deduction for absent days
- More than 3 consecutive days may lead to termination proceedings

### Repeated Tardiness
- Warning after 3 instances in a month
- Written warning after repeat in consecutive months
- Potential performance review impact

## Special Provisions

### New Parents
- Flexible hours for first 6 months after child birth/adoption
- Work from home options available
- Nursing mothers: Extended lunch breaks permitted

### Medical Conditions
- Employees with medical conditions can request accommodations
- Medical certificate from registered practitioner required
- HR and supervisor to work out suitable arrangement
