# Company Leave Policy

## Overview
This document outlines the leave policy for all employees at TechCorp Inc. All employees are entitled to various types of leave as described below.

## Leave Types and Entitlements

### 1. Casual Leave
- **Annual Entitlement**: 15 days per year
- **Purpose**: Personal matters, family functions, or other short-term needs
- **Carryforward**: Up to 5 days can be carried forward to the next year
- **Advance Notice**: Minimum 2 days advance notice required
- **Approval**: Requires immediate supervisor approval

### 2. Sick Leave
- **Annual Entitlement**: 10 days per year
- **Purpose**: Illness, medical appointments, or recovery
- **Medical Certificate**: Required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days
- **Carryforward**: Up to 10 days can be accumulated (maximum 20 days total)
- **Advance Notice**: Should be communicated as soon as possible
- **Approval**: Requires immediate supervisor approval

### 3. Earned Leave (Privileged Leave)
- **Annual Entitlement**: 12 days per year
- **Purpose**: Extended vacation or personal time off
- **Accrual**: Earned monthly (1 day per month)
- **Carryforward**: Unlimited carryforward
- **Advance Notice**: Minimum 15 days advance notice required for more than 3 consecutive days
- **Approval**: Requires department head approval for leaves exceeding 5 days

## Leave Application Process

### How to Apply for Leave

1. **Submit Application**: Use the HR portal or contact HR assistant at least 2 days in advance
2. **Provide Details**:
   - Start date and end date
   - Type of leave (casual, sick, or earned)
   - Reason for leave
   - Contact information during leave

3. **Await Approval**: Your supervisor will review and approve/reject within 24 hours
4. **Receive Confirmation**: You'll receive email confirmation once approved

### Emergency Leave
In case of emergencies:
- Inform your supervisor immediately by phone or email
- Submit formal application within 24 hours of returning
- Provide supporting documentation if required

## Leave Balance
- Employees can check their leave balance through:
  - HR portal
  - Monthly payslip
  - HR assistant chatbot
  - Direct inquiry to HR department

## Public Holidays
All official public holidays are separate from personal leave entitlements. Employees are not required to use their leave for public holidays.

## Probation Period
Employees on probation (first 3 months) are entitled to:
- Sick leave: Available from day one
- Casual leave: Available after 1 month
- Earned leave: Starts accruing from day one, usable after probation

## Important Notes
1. Leave applications submitted without proper advance notice may be rejected
2. Falsification of leave reasons may result in disciplinary action
3. Unused casual leave (beyond carryforward limit) will lapse at year-end
4. Leave encashment available only for earned leave (subject to company policy)
5. Maximum consecutive leave: 30 days (requires special approval)
